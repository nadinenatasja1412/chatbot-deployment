About ChildHealth Monitor

ChildHealth Monitor is an AI-first platform dedicated to eradicating child malnutrition. We combine anthropometric standards, community-collected data, and advanced machine learning models from `app.py` to deliver reliable assessments through the lightweight Flask bridge `api_server.py`.

Field-ready dashboards empower ministries of health, NGOs, and community workers to act quickly with confidence, while a secure account portal keeps family records safe.

  • Predictive models trained on WHO standard Z-scores
  • Offline-ready mobile capture kits
  • Actionable insights ranked by urgency
  • Privacy-first architecture
